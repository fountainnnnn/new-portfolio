Quick answers before you jump into the predictor.

23x23 is optimized for speed and quick feedback. 101x101 keeps more detail and tends to perform better when texture and edges matter (leafy greens, cluttered backgrounds, partial occlusion).

If the top confidence is below a threshold (or the two models disagree), the UI flags the result as uncertain. It's a heuristic "is this even a vegetable?" gate to encourage better inputs.
